projects

MetrOne is a simple mobile web application that allows user to reserve their time when they visit to a specific store in a mall. It was completed in a team of 4 over the course of 5 weeks during our first semester at BCIT. We utilizes HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Javascript for the front-end, and Firebase as our databse. We also emphasizes collaboration tools using Github, Agile, and Sprints.

Lizzy is a list-keeper bot. She will take care of your to-watch movies, games to play, and a server other activities. With simple built-in commands group members can add new list, and add or remove items to and from it. Lists can be accessed later without having to scroll up use the search function.

It was completed individually over the course of three days. Some technologies and concepts that I used are Discord.js, NodeJS, JavaScript, and Object-Oriented Fundamentals.

DisConvo is a webapp where users have disagreeing conversations based on ideas they disagree with. It allows the user to see from others' perspective and have ideas in mind about the opposing side.

It was completed with a group of 4 over the course of 24-hours. Some technologies and I used to contribute with this projects are: JavaScript, Node.Js, SocketIO, and HTML, CSS.
